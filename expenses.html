Expense claim form

Use this form to claim reimbursement for work-related expenses paid from your own pocket. All claims require Chief Executive approval before payment.

Expense policy summary

  • Expenses must be incurred wholly and necessarily in the course of your duties at The Station Richmond
  • All items over £5 must be supported by a receipt — claims without receipts may be declined
  • Expenses should be submitted within 30 days of the purchase date
  • Personal items, alcohol (unless pre-approved for a specific event), and non-work purchases are not claimable
  • Claims must be approved by the Chief Executive before reimbursement is made
  • Reimbursement is paid into your wages bank account alongside your next pay run
  • Any expense over £50 should be agreed with the Chief Executive before the purchase where possible
